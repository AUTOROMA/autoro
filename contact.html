Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our professional car rental platform.

How do I rent a car on Autoro?

Sign up, verify your account, search for available cars from verified agencies, and submit a booking request. Once confirmed by the rental agency, complete payment and pick up your rental. Learn more about how it works.

How do I register my rental agency?

Create an agency account, complete business verification with your license, add your fleet details with photos, set your pricing and availability, then activate your listings. See the complete guide for rental agencies.

Is insurance included when I rent a car?

Yes, every rental on our professional car rental platform includes comprehensive insurance covering liability, collision, and theft provided by licensed agencies.

How does payment work?

Customers pay securely through our car rental platform. Funds are held in escrow and released to rental agencies after pickup. All transactions are protected with bank-level security.

What if I have an issue during my rental?

Contact our 24/7 support team immediately at support@autoro.ma or +212 5XX-XXXXXX. We provide roadside assistance and emergency support for all active rentals on our car rental app.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel according to our cancellation policy. The earlier you cancel, the higher your refund. Check specific cancellation terms when you book to rent a car.

What are the requirements to rent a car?

You must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license held for 1+ year, government ID, and a payment method. View complete renter requirements.

What are the benefits of joining as a rental agency?

Rental agencies gain access to thousands of customers, professional booking management tools, and integrated payment systems. Grow your business and expand your reach across Morocco. See agency benefits.
