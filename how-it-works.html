How Autoro Works

Our car rental app makes professional car rental simple, safe, and rewarding for everyone in Morocco.

Two Ways to Use Autoro

Whether you want to rent a car from professional agencies or register your rental agency, our car rental platform has you covered.

For Renters: How to Rent a Car

Find and book the perfect car rental in Morocco with our easy-to-use car rental app.

Create Your Account

Sign up on our car rental platform with your email and phone number. Complete your profile with a valid driver's license and verification documents to ensure safe professional car rental for everyone.

Search for Available Cars

Browse our wide selection of vehicles available to rent a car in Morocco. Filter by location, car type, price range, and dates. Each listing includes detailed photos, specifications, and reviews from previous renters.

Review Car Details

Check the car's features, rental terms, insurance coverage, and owner requirements. Read reviews from other users who have used this car on our car rental platform.

Request to Book

Select your rental dates and submit a booking request through our car rental app. The car owner will review your profile and respond within 24 hours.

Complete Payment Securely

Once approved, complete your payment through our secure platform. Your payment is protected and only released to the owner after you pick up the car.

Pick Up Your Rental Car

Meet the car owner at the agreed location to pick up your rental. Inspect the car together and document its condition before you rent a car and hit the road.

Enjoy Your Trip

Drive with peace of mind knowing you have comprehensive insurance coverage and 24/7 support from our car rental platform throughout your journey.

Return and Review

Return the car on time in the same condition. Leave a review to help other renters on our professional car rental platform make informed decisions.

For Rental Agencies: How to List Your Car

Turn your idle vehicle into a source of passive income on Morocco's leading car rental platform.

Sign Up as a Car Owner

Create your account on our car rental app and complete the verification process. Provide your ID, driver's license, and vehicle registration documents.

List Your Vehicle

Add your car to our professional car rental platform. Upload high-quality photos showing your vehicle from multiple angles. Include detailed information about make, model, year, features, and condition.

Set Your Pricing and Availability

Choose your daily and weekly rental rates. Our car rental platform provides pricing recommendations based on your car type and local market rates in Morocco. Set your calendar to show when your car is available to rent.

Define Your Requirements

Set minimum renter age, driving experience requirements, and any special conditions for your car rental. You have full control over who can rent your car on our car rental platform.

Review Rental Requests

Receive notifications when renters want to book your car. Review their profile, driving history, and ratings before approving or declining requests on our car rental app.

Hand Over Your Car

Meet the verified renter at the agreed location. Walk through the car together, document its condition with photos, and hand over the keys. Your car is now covered by comprehensive insurance.

Earn Passive Income

Get paid automatically when the rental period begins. Funds are transferred to your account securely through our professional car rental platform.

Receive Your Car Back

The renter returns your car at the scheduled time. Inspect it together and confirm the return on our car rental app. Leave a review to help other owners on the car rental platform.

Safety and Trust on Our Car Rental Platform

We take security seriously to ensure safe professional car rental experiences for everyone.

Identity Verification

All users on our car rental platform undergo thorough verification. We check driver's licenses, government IDs, and phone numbers to ensure you rent a car from or to verified users only.

Comprehensive Insurance

Every car rental includes liability coverage, collision damage waiver, and theft protection. Both rental agencies and renters are fully protected on our car rental app.

Secure Payments

All transactions on our professional car rental platform are processed securely. Payments are held in escrow and only released after successful car pickup.

24/7 Support

Our customer support team is available around the clock to assist with any issues during your car rental experience in Morocco.

User Reviews and Ratings

Our car rental platform features a transparent review system. Make informed decisions based on real experiences from other renters and rental agencies.

Roadside Assistance

Every rental includes 24/7 roadside assistance. If you need help while you rent a car, we're just a phone call away.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car on our platform, you need a valid driver's license, government-issued ID, and a payment method. Some rental agencies on our professional car rental platform may have additional requirements.

Is insurance included?

Yes, comprehensive insurance coverage is included with every car rental on our car rental platform, protecting both renters and rental agencies.

How do payments work?

Pay securely through our car rental app. Funds are held in escrow and released to the car owner after you pick up the vehicle, ensuring safe professional car rental transactions.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel according to our cancellation policy. The earlier you cancel, the more refund you receive. Check the specific terms when you rent a car.
