Frequently Asked Questions

Is my car insured during rentals?

Yes, comprehensive insurance coverage is included with every rental on our car rental platform at no cost to you. This includes liability, collision, and theft protection.

How much does it cost to list?

Listing your fleet on our professional car rental platform is completely free. We only take a small service fee when your fleet is rented, so you only pay when you earn.

Can I choose who rents my car?

Absolutely. You review every rental request on our car rental app and can approve or decline based on renter profiles, ratings, and your preferences.

When do I get paid?

Payments are processed automatically by our car rental platform and transferred to your bank account within 24 hours after the rental begins.

What if a renter damages my car?

Our insurance covers damage costs. File a claim through the car rental app, and we'll process it within 48 hours. You'll also receive compensation for loss of use.

Can I remove my listing anytime?

Yes, you have complete control on our professional car rental platform. Pause or remove your listing whenever you want, as long as you honor existing confirmed bookings.
