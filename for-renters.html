Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I need to be to rent a car?

You must be at least 21 years old to rent a car on our professional car rental platform. Some premium vehicles may require renters to be 25 or older.

Is insurance included when I rent a car?

Yes, comprehensive insurance is included with every rental on our car rental platform at no extra cost. This covers liability, collision, and theft.

How does payment work?

Pay securely through our car rental app. Your payment is held in escrow and released to the owner only after you pick up the car.

Can I rent a car for just a few hours?

Minimum rental periods vary by owner on our professional car rental platform. Many cars are available for half-day or full-day rentals.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel your booking on our car rental platform according to the cancellation policy. Earlier cancellations receive higher refunds.

Is there mileage limit when I rent a car?

Mileage limits are set by individual rental agencies on our car rental platform. Many offer unlimited mileage, while others specify daily limits.

What happens if the car breaks down?

Call our 24/7 roadside assistance immediately. We'll send help and arrange a replacement if needed when you rent a car through our professional car rental platform.

Can someone else drive the car?

Additional drivers can be added on our car rental platform for a small fee. They must also meet verification requirements and be approved by the owner.
